The Egyptian Basketball Arbitration Duo obtains the license "B" from the International Federation

The duo Mohammed Al-Damiati and Sarah Jamal, the two international basketball referees, obtained the "B" license from the International Game Federation. The license came after they were promoted in 3x3 competitions from the International Basketball Association.

The Alexandria Basketball Federation team qualified for the Super League Championship final after defeating its Zamalek counterpart with a score of 108/97 in the third round of the semi-finals of the Basketball League, to escalate to the championship final. The Union qualification came after winning 3 out of 5 games according to the tournament system Which takes place with the best of 5 system, with 5 matches between teams.

Thus, the next matches between the two teams that were scheduled for next Saturday will not be held, and the Alexandrian Federation is scheduled to meet the winner of the Al-Ahly and Al-Jazira match, which was scheduled for Thursday, but was postponed due to bad weather conditions.

The Basketball Association decided to postpone all activities and matches scheduled for Thursday, especially the Al-Ahly and Al-Jazira match in the third round of BEST OF 5 matches. The Federation also decided to postpone the 3/3 festival, which was its residence on Thursday, Friday and Saturday, due to bad weather conditions too, The Basketball Association is scheduled to resume its activities next week, naturally.
