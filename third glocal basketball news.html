Celtics stars Jayson Tatum and Jaylen Brown is awesome

The Boston Celtics have two of the best young stars in the NBA, and these players engaged in a fun 1-on-1 game after practice Tuesday. Jayson Tatum and Jaylen Brown both are in the midst of career seasons for the Celtics. Tatum made his first All-Star appearance and won Eastern Conference Player of the Month in February, while Brown is averaging a career-high 20 points per game and playing exceptional perimeter defense.

Tatum and Brown sharpened their skills in a game of 1-on-1 in the Orlando bubble yesterday, and it provided some fantastic footage.
