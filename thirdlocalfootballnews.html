Carteron meets with the staff and team doctor at Borg El Arab camp

Last Updated: 08:10 21/07/2020




French coach Patrice Carteron, coach of the first football team in the Zamalek club, was keen to meet with his assistant staff, Ahmed Abdel-Raouf, Amir Abdel-Aziz and Amr Abdel-Salam, inside the team camp currently held in Borg El-Arab.


Carteron discussed with his auxiliary apparatus some matters related to the camp and the players training program, as well as asking them to follow up on all the players and the extent of each player's commitment to the specific training program, and to submit reports to him continuously.


"Carteron" met Dr. Mohamed Osama, the team's doctor, and the rest of the medical staff, to check on the status of all players and their readiness to take part in the training, within the framework of the specific program from the technical staff to prepare the players for the next period.


The team started its camp last Thursday, in Borg El Arab, Alexandria, in preparation for contesting the African Champions League semi-final against Moroccan Raja.
