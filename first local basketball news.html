The Basketball Association announces the winners of the creative thought competition

The Basketball Association, headed by Magdy Abu Fraikha, announced the results of the competition organized by the Federation to develop the coaches for the stage for the mini-basket, which was supervised by an evaluation committee for the federation, and the result of the winners was Mahmoud Abdel-Latif, who won the first position, and Ayman Halim ranked second, while Khaled Idris ranked the third .

Ayman Halim, Mahmoud Abdel Latif, Mohamed Mamdouh Al-Jaafari, in addition to Amr Radwan and Khaled Idris, participated in the competition through the official page on the Internet, with the feature of the video, and they presented and explained their project to develop the creative thinking of the coaches.

The idea of the competition came to break the state of boredom in light of the curfew, and activity stopped, and it is scheduled to resume gradually in mid-June.

The Basketball Association, headed by Magdy Abu Freikha, spent, during the past days, 10 thousand pounds for each region of the game at the level of the Republic to buy hygiene tools in light of the outbreak of the Corona virus. , Which amounts to 10 thousand pounds for each region at the level of the Republic, to purchase hygiene and sterilization supplies and tools, to avoid infection with the emerging corona virus.
