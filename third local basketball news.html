Basket of Union renew Ahmed Sabbagh's contracts for a new season

The board of directors of Al-Ittihad Alexandria club succeeded in renewing the contract with Ahmed Al-Sabbagh, the star of the team for a new season, to continue his contract until 2020/2021, based on the desire of Ahmed Marai, the coach of the first team for his part. And that there are more than one player who are the main pillars of the basket leader, including Al-Sabbagh, Mohammed Abdel-Muttalib, Vice President of the Basketball Association revealed that the intention of the officials of the Federation is to complete the season with all its competitions, which came out and was announced by Magdy Abu Farkhah, President of the Basketball Association before, According to the Ministry of Youth and Sports, for his part, President of the Basketball Association, Magdy Abu Fraikha, affirmed that the federation is awaiting the instructions of the Ministry of Youth and Sports regarding the precautionary and preventive measures to be followed for the return of sports activity in mid-June, according to what the ministry announced in implementation of the cabinet decision, that The return of activity will be gradual. Abu Freikha added that he will not be able to determine the steps that the federation will follow until after the instructions of the ministry are reached, especially since there are remaining matches in the sporting season of the game that decide the league champion.
